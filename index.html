About Rod's Boat & Marine Repairs

Located in Mount Druitt, NSW, Rod's Boat & Marine Repairs has been serving the Sydney boating community for over a decade. We specialize in comprehensive boat repairs, engine maintenance, and custom upgrades to keep your vessel sailing smoothly.

Our certified technicians use state-of-the-art tools to ensure quality service every time. Whether it's a routine check-up or major repair, we're committed to excellence and customer satisfaction.
