About Rod's Boat & Marine Repairs


Established in Mount Druitt, NSW, Rod's Boat & Marine Repairs offers top-tier services for boat owners across Sydney. With over 10 years of experience, our certified team handles everything from routine maintenance to complex repairs and custom upgrades.
